Primary surface container — navy fill with a hairline ring (no heavy shadow). Composes session cards, product cards, need rows and forms.

```jsx
<Card interactive pad>
  <div className="ros-card__title">Upgrade robotics fab</div>
  <CardDescription>Jun 6 · goal-driven · $4,200 budget</CardDescription>
</Card>
```

Sub-parts: `CardHeader`, `CardTitle`, `CardDescription`, `CardContent`, `CardFooter`. Use `interactive` for clickable cards (hover lifts to surface-3). Use `pad`/`padSm` for quick padding or compose your own with the sub-parts.
