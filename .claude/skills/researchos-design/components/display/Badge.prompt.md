Compact pill for status, category, and risk tags.

```jsx
<Badge variant="success">complete</Badge>
<Badge variant="info" mono>SEARCHING</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">no datasheet</Badge>
```

Semantic map: `success`=complete/strong, `warning`=evaluating/partial, `danger`=failed/poor, `info`=searching/analyzing, `brand`=decided. Use `mono` to switch to the uppercase technical voice.
